Order of the Lion Guild Manager v1.4.1

Guild companion created specifically for Order of the Lion on OctoWoW.

Main features:
- Improved Guild and Officer chat.
- Guild roster, professions, ranks, activity and Guild Information.
- PvE Hub with red Raid Alerts, leader-created Group Finder groups and a shared Guild Board.
- Direct addon-to-addon synchronization between online guildmates.
- Raider/Core Raider-only raid reminder popups.
- Officer recruitment, history and inactive-member tools.

Group Finder:
- A leader creates a group and chooses the activity, group size and needed roles.
- Other players send a Join Request with their role and optional note.
- The leader can Accept + Invite, Decline or Whisper candidates.
- Groups and applications expire after 60 minutes.

Addon network:
- Addon users are detected from all valid addon traffic, including PvE sync messages.
- The counter excludes the current character and shows other detected users online.

Commands:
/otl - open or close the addon
/otltest - diagnostics
